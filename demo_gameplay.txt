INCREMENTAL DUNGEON CRAWLER - GAMEPLAY DEMONSTRATION
====================================================

This file demonstrates the key features of the game through example output.

MAIN MENU
---------
The player starts with basic stats and can choose from several options:
- Enter Dungeon (explore and fight)
- Upgrade Stats (spend gold)
- View Statistics (check progress)
- Save/Load Game (persistence)

Starting Stats:
  Level: 1 | HP: 100/100
  Attack: 10 | Defense: 5
  Gold: 0 | EXP: 0/100
  Floors Cleared: 0 | Dungeons: 0

BIOME SELECTION
---------------
5 Different Biomes Available:
1. Forest - Classic fantasy forest with goblins and wolves
2. Cave - Dark underground with bats and golems
3. Desert - Sandy wasteland with scorpions and mummies
4. Ice Cavern - Frozen caves with yetis and ice dragons
5. Volcano - Fiery depths with lava golems and phoenixes

DUNGEON SIZE SELECTION
---------------------
4 Different Sizes:
1. Small (5 floors, 1x difficulty) - Good for beginners
2. Medium (10 floors, 1.5x difficulty) - Moderate challenge
3. Large (20 floors, 2x difficulty) - For experienced players
4. Epic (50 floors, 3x difficulty) - Maximum challenge

COMBAT SYSTEM
-------------
Turn-based combat with:
- Damage calculation: Your Attack - Enemy Defense (minimum 1)
- Counter-attack: Enemy deals damage back each turn
- Progressive difficulty: Enemies get stronger each floor
- Boss fights: Final floor has a powerful boss with 3x rewards

Example Combat Flow:
  Player attacks Goblin for 7 damage (10 attack - 3 defense)
  Goblin counter-attacks for 3 damage (8 attack - 5 defense)
  Player HP: 100 -> 97
  Goblin HP: 50 -> 43

PROGRESSION MECHANICS
--------------------
1. Experience & Leveling:
   - Kill enemies to earn XP
   - Level up to gain stats automatically
   - Level 1->2 requires 100 XP, then 150, 225, etc. (1.5x)

2. Gold & Upgrades:
   - Earn gold from defeating enemies
   - Spend gold on permanent stat upgrades
   - Upgrade costs scale exponentially (1.5x per purchase)

3. Floor Healing:
   - Heal 30% of max HP between floors
   - Full heal when starting new dungeon
   - Full heal on death (return to town)

AUTO-BATTLE MODE
----------------
Toggle auto-battle to:
- Automatically attack each turn
- Speed up grinding and farming
- Still see results of each fight
- Can disable anytime

SAVE/LOAD SYSTEM
----------------
- Save progress to save_game.json
- Persist all stats, gold, experience
- Track total achievements
- Resume exactly where you left off

UPGRADE SYSTEM
--------------
Three upgrade types:
1. Max Health +20 (Cost: 50 gold base)
2. Attack +5 (Cost: 100 gold base)
3. Defense +2 (Cost: 80 gold base)

Costs increase with each purchase!

ENDGAME LOOP
------------
1. Clear small dungeons to gain levels and gold
2. Upgrade your stats with earned gold
3. Tackle larger dungeons for better rewards
4. Try different biomes for variety
5. Push for epic dungeons and boss completions
6. Build the ultimate character!

BOSS ENCOUNTERS
---------------
Final floor of each dungeon:
- Boss enemy with unique name (e.g., "Forest Boss")
- 2.5x health, 1.5x attack/defense
- 3x gold and experience rewards
- Greater challenge, greater rewards!

ACHIEVEMENTS TO TRACK
--------------------
- Total Floors Cleared
- Total Dungeons Completed
- Maximum Level Reached
- Gold Accumulated
- Each Biome Mastered

EXAMPLE PROGRESSION PATH
------------------------
1. Start: Level 1, 100 HP, 10 ATK, 5 DEF
2. Clear Small Forest dungeon (5 floors)
3. Earn ~50-100 gold and level up to 2-3
4. Upgrade Attack once
5. Try Medium Cave dungeon (10 floors)
6. Continue upgrading and leveling
7. Eventually tackle Epic Volcano dungeon!

The game is designed for incremental progress where each run makes you
stronger, allowing you to tackle increasingly difficult content.

Enjoy the adventure!
